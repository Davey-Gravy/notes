#### Compound Interest

Loan *L* and interest rate *r*
$$
x(0)=L
$$
Compound yearly:
$$
x(1)=(1+r)L=(1+r)\,x(0)
$$
Compound monthly:
$$
x(1)=\left(1+\frac{r}{12}\right)^{12}x(0)
$$
$$
\left(1+\frac{r}{12}\right>^{12}1+r
$$
As compound period approaches zero:
$$
\begin{align}
x(1)&=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\left(1+\frac{r}{n}\right)^nx(0)\\
&= e^rx(0)
\end{align}
$$
